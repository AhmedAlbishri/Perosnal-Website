About

I am an Assistant Professor at Saudi Electronic University, College of Computing and Informatics, Information Technology Department, and serve as Vice-Dean for Curriculum Development at the Deanship of E-Learning. My research and professional work focus on Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Educational Technology.

My Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Missouri - Kansas City specialized in Artificial Intelligence for Computer Vision, with particular emphasis on medical domain applications. This foundation in AI and computer vision has enabled me to apply my expertise across multiple domains and projects, from medical imaging to educational technology.

I am passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and enhance educational experiences. Through my research, teaching, and development work, I develop innovative tools and systems that improve learning outcomes and make technology more accessible in academic and professional settings.
